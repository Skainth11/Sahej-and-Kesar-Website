What is Robo-Walk?
Robo-Walk is a wearable robotic treadmill that lets you move naturally in place — perfect for gaming, fitness, and remote exploration.
Travel Mode
Navigate your environment with ease — glide across campuses, offices, or convention floors using your body’s natural stride.
Exercise Mode
Burn calories in a small space. Use Robo-Walk to simulate long-distance walks, jogs, or runs — all while staying in one spot.
App Control
Use our mobile app to control speed, switch modes, customize lighting, and track your performance stats in real-time.
Rechargeable Battery
With up to 5 hours of battery life and fast USB-C charging, Robo-Walk is ready for all-day adventures or workouts.
Built for the Future
From VR compatibility to AI-assisted movement correction, Robo-Walk is built to evolve with the next generation of motion tech.
