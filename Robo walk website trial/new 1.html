Robo-Walk

The Future of Personal Transportation

What is Robo-Walk?

Robo-Walk is a self-moving electric vehicle, an alternative to bikes and scooters. It offers two modes: Exercise Mode (static) and Travel Mode (automatic movement).

Key Features

  • **Exercise Mode** – Stay fit by disabling movement.
  • **Travel Mode** – Move around effortlessly.
  • **Smart Connectivity** – Wi-Fi enabled for real-time navigation.
  • **Custom App** – Change color, speed, and settings.
  • **Smart Paint** – ChromaFlair technology for color-changing effects.

Who Would Buy Robo-Walk?

Perfect for students and commuters looking for an efficient, eco-friendly alternative to electric bikes and scooters.

How Robo-Walk Helps in Schools

Designed to reduce hallway congestion, Robo-Walk units communicate via Wi-Fi to avoid collisions.

Our Competitors
